	Fundamental Canons Of Ethics

1. Engineers shall hold paramount the safety, health and welfare of public.

2. Engineers shall perform the services only in the areas of the competence.

3. Engineers shall issue public statements only in an objective and truthful manner.

4. Engineers shall act in professional matters for each employer as faithful agents.

5. Engineers shall build their professional reputation on the merit of their services.

6. Engineers shall  act in a manner to uphold and enhance the honour of the profession.

7. Engineers shall continue their professional development throughout the career.
